# Ekman Microexpressions (Lie Detection)

**Key Signs of Deception**:
- **Microexpressions** (1/25 sec): Fear (raised brows AU1+2, furrowed AU4), Anger (AU4 lips tight), Disgust (AU9 nose wrinkle).
- **Asymmetrical smiles** (duping delight).
- **Eye contact avoidance** + overcompensation.
- **Illustrators mismatch** (gestures don't match words).
- **Baseline deviation**: Compare to normal behavior.
- **Clusters**: 3+ signs = high prob.

Prob: Isolated=20%, Cluster=70-90%.
