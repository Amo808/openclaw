# Cipolla Stupidity Laws

Types:
- **Intelligent**: Wins, others neutral/loss.
- **Bandit**: Wins at others' loss.
- **Helpless**: Loses, others neutral/win.
- **Fool**: Hurts others AND self.

Law 1: All overestimate intelligence.
2: Probability fool = constant.
3: Fool > bandit threat.
4: Non-fools underestimate fools.
5: Fool seeks max damage, esp. to non-fools.

Classify: Foolish behavior = high risk.
