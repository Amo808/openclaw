# Leonhard-Lichko Psychotypes

- **Paranoid**: Suspicious, jealous, vengeful, interprets neutral as threat. High loyalty to 'us', hates 'them'.
- **Epileptoid**: Pedantic, orderly, power-hungry, explosive anger, values possessions/control.
- **Schizoid**: Introverted, fantasy-world, aloof, poor emotions, original ideas.
- **Hypotim**: Pessimistic, low energy, passive, self-doubting, reliable but slow.
- **Hyperthym**: Energetic, sociable, risk-taking, impulsive, charming but superficial.
- **Hysteroid**: Attention-seeking, dramatic, suggestible, volatile moods.
- **Unstable**: Cyclothymic, mood swings, dependent.
- **Conformal**: Follower, adapts to group, avoids conflict.
- **Astheno-neurotic**: Anxious, fatigable, hypochondriac.
